# Poster Design Rules — Sacon Global Sports Park (Outdoor Wall Poster)

## 1. Context & Purpose

- **Type**: Outdoor wall poster for the entrance wall of Sacon Global Sports Park
- **Goal**: Showcase gym amenities and signify **luxury / premium** positioning
- **Brand**: Thrust Fit (formerly referenced as Sacon Global Sports Park)
- **Identity**: THRUST FIT (premium gym brand)
- **Location reference**: Vignan Nagar

---

## 2. Dimensions

| Format | Width | Height | Aspect Ratio |
|--------|-------|--------|--------------|
| Physical print | 10 ft | 8 ft | 5:4 (landscape) |
| HTML mockup (scaled) | 1200px | 960px | 5:4 (landscape) |

> **IMPORTANT**: A **print-ready version** (high-res, CMYK-ready, bleed margins) will be needed later — keep all assets and layout scalable.

---

## 3. Layout & Composition

### Split Composition
- **Left half (~50%)**: Hero imagery of athletes/gym-goers with a dark semi-transparent overlay for text readability
- **Right half (~50%)**: Bright, aspirational gym interior — floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking green sports fields
- The two halves blend naturally — no hard dividing line

### Content Zones (Top to Bottom)

| Zone | Content | Position |
|------|---------|----------|
| Top-right corner | Brand logo (Thrust Fit) | Fixed, top-right |
| Upper-left | Main headline | Large, left-aligned |
| Mid-left | Body copy / description paragraph | Below headline, left-aligned, narrower width (~40%) |
| Bottom span | "THRUST" watermark text | Full-width, bottom area, semi-transparent |
| Bottom center | Tagline: "Train. Recover. Perform." | Centered, overlaid on watermark |
| Bottom-right | Decorative star/diamond accent | Near bottom-right |

---

## 4. Typography

### Hierarchy

| Element | Style | Weight | Size (relative) | Case |
|---------|-------|--------|-----------------|------|
| Headline | Serif or elegant sans-serif | Bold / Black | Very large (~80-100px at mockup scale) | UPPERCASE |
| Body copy | Clean sans-serif | Regular / Light | Small (~14-16px at mockup scale) | Sentence case |
| Watermark "THRUST" | Bold display font | Black / Extra Bold | Massive (~200px+), semi-transparent | UPPERCASE |
| Tagline | Serif or elegant sans-serif | Bold | Medium (~28-32px at mockup scale) | Title Case with periods |
| Logo text | Brand font | — | Small | As per brand |

### Font Recommendations (Elegant / Premium)
- **Headlines & Tagline**: `Playfair Display`, `Cormorant Garamond`, or `Didot` — classic serif for luxury feel
- **Body copy**: `Inter`, `Outfit`, or `Montserrat` — clean, modern sans-serif
- **Watermark**: `Oswald`, `Bebas Neue`, or bold condensed sans-serif

> All fonts must convey **elegance and premium quality**. Avoid playful, rounded, or overly casual typefaces.

## 5. Color Palette

| Role | Color | Hex (approx) | Usage |
|------|-------|--------------|-------|
| Primary dark | Charcoal / near-black | `#1a1a1a` — `#2d2d2d` | Overlay on left side, text background |
| Text primary | White | `#FFFFFF` | Headlines, tagline |
| Text secondary | Light gray | `#d4d4d4` — `#e0e0e0` | Body copy |
| Watermark | Dark teal / slate | `#2a3a3a` — `#3d5050` | "THRUST" text, low opacity |
| Accent | Gold / warm tone | `#c8a96e` or similar | Optional accent for premium feel |
| Background right | Natural / bright | — | Derived from gym interior photo |

### Overlay
- Left side uses a **dark gradient overlay** (left: ~80% opacity → right: ~20% opacity) to ensure text readability while blending into the right-side image

## 6. Imagery

### Provided Image Assets
1. **hero-transparent.jpeg** — Three athletes (2 men, 1 woman) on black background — composited over left side
2. **bg.jpeg** — Modern premium gym interior with floor-to-ceiling glass windows, palm trees, sports fields (right side)
3. **logo.svg** — Thrust Fit brand logo (SVG format)

### Image Treatment
- Left image: desaturated slightly, darkened with overlay
- Right image: bright, natural colors — aspirational and airy
- Images should blend seamlessly at the center

---

## 7. Design Principles

1. **Luxury & Premium**: Every element must reinforce a high-end, exclusive feel
2. **Elegance over excess**: Clean layouts, generous whitespace, refined typography
3. **Aspirational tone**: The imagery and copy should inspire confidence and exclusivity
4. **Readability at distance**: This is an outdoor wall poster — text must be legible from a distance
5. **Brand consistency**: Logo placement, brand colors, and tone must align with Thrust Fit identity

---

## 8. Decorative Elements

- **Star / Diamond accent** (✦): Positioned near bottom-right as a subtle decorative element
- **No borders or frames**: The design should feel open and expansive
- Minimal use of graphic elements — let typography and photography do the heavy lifting

## 10. Print-Ready Considerations (For Later)

> These will be addressed after the HTML mockup is approved.

- Output resolution: **300 DPI minimum**
- Color mode: **CMYK**
- Bleed: **0.25 inches** on all sides
- Safe zone: **0.5 inches** inset from trim
- File format: **PDF/X-1a** or high-res **TIFF**
- All fonts should be outlined/embedded
- Images must be high-resolution (minimum 150 DPI at print size)

---

## 11. Tone & Voice

- **Confident** — assertive, not aggressive
- **Premium** — exclusive, not elitist
- **Professional** — polished, not corporate
- **Aspirational** — inspiring, not intimidating
